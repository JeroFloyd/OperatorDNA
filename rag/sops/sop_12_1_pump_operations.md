# SOP 12.1 — Centrifugal Pump Operations

**Revision:** 3.8
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-20
**Applicable To:** Process operators, maintenance technicians

## Purpose

Standard operating procedure for starting, stopping, and controlling centrifugal pumps in process service.

## Normal Operating Range

- **Normal RPM:** 1200-1800 RPM
- **Minimum:** 500 RPM (thermal protection limit)
- **Maximum:** 2000 RPM (design limit)
- **Normal Discharge Pressure:** 1.2-1.8 bar

## Start Procedure

1. Verify suction valve is fully open
2. Verify discharge valve is 20% open
3. Start pump at minimum speed (500 RPM)
4. Gradually increase to 1200 RPM over 10 seconds
5. Verify discharge pressure stabilizes
6. Adjust to target flow rate

## Stop Procedure

1. Gradually reduce RPM to 500 over 15 seconds
2. Close discharge valve
3. Stop pump
4. Verify zero flow

## Abnormal Conditions

### Cavitation (erratic pressure, noise)
- Reduce RPM by 200-300
- Check suction strainer
- Verify NPSH available

### Overload (high motor current)
- Reduce RPM immediately
- Check for closed discharge valve
- Verify fluid specific gravity

### High Vibration
- Reduce RPM to minimum
- Check for cavitation
- Notify maintenance

## Emergency Stop

- Press E-stop button
- Close discharge valve
- Report immediately to shift supervisor
